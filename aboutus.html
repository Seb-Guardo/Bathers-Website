Welcome to Bathers Coogee, where flavors from around the world come together in a celebration of love and culture. Founded by a passionate migrant couple, Peter and Athena, our restaurant is a labor of love, born from a desire to share our heritage through exquisite cuisine.


With Greece as our inspiration, each dish tells a story of tradition, family, and the vibrant tapestry of flavors that define our roots. From aromatic spices to time-honored recipes passed down through generations, every bite is a journey across continents.


At Bathers Coogee, we strive to create not just a dining experience but a warm embrace of diverse cultures. Our cozy ambiance invites you to savor each moment, surrounded by the rich aromas and the heartfelt hospitality that define our establishment.


Join us on this culinary adventure as we blend passion with tradition, creating a dining experience that transcends borders and welcomes all to share in our journey. We invite you to become part of our story and indulge in the flavors of home, wherever that may be.
